Biography

    Gonglong Chen is currently a third year PhD student at the College of Computer Science in Zhejiang University. He received the BS degree from the College of Computer Science in East China University of Political Science and Law (ECUPL) in 2011. His research interests include Internet of Things (IoT) and wireless and mobile computing.
